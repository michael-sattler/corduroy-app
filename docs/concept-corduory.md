# Problem

Small and mid-sized businesses \- which make up the vast majority of the economy \- face a strategic advisory gap that leaves them chronically underserved. On one side are traditional consulting firms: expensive, episodic, and disconnected from day-to-day reality. They parachute in, produce a polished slide deck, and disappear. On the other side are generic SaaS tools that surface data and metrics but offer no interpretation, no prioritization, and no path forward.

The result is a painful paradox: the businesses that most need strategic guidance are precisely the ones who can least afford it. SMB leaders are left making high-stakes decisions \- about hiring, growth, pricing, operations \- without the frameworks, benchmarks, or outside perspective that their larger competitors take for granted.

This isn't a niche problem. It is the defining operational challenge for tens of millions of businesses worldwide, and it compounds over time: without clear strategy, execution suffers; without execution, growth stalls; without growth, the business falls further behind.

# Solution

Wouldn't it be transformative if something could continuously learn the specifics of a business \- its financials, its operations, its goals \- and then act like a seasoned strategic advisor available around the clock? Not just surfacing data, but interpreting it. Not just generating reports, but translating them into a concrete, prioritized, week-by-week action plan. And then staying with the business every single day \- reminding, coaching, adapting \- so that the strategy actually gets executed rather than filed away.

The ideal solution would combine the depth and personalization of expert human consulting with the scale, consistency, and affordability of modern software \- and it would be designed specifically for the realities of small and mid-sized business, not retrofitted from enterprise tools.

# Product

Corduroy uses the company’s data sources that reflect a company’s behavior to detect patterns and derive true behavioral intelligence. Behavior is a methodology that identifies the ongoing actions (“behavioral signals”) a business engages in that result in business outcomes, and helps adjust those behaviors to achieve desired business outcomes

Corduroy is an AI-enabled behavioral intelligence platform built for small and mid-sized businesses. It combines expert human knowledge and oversight with the analytical horsepower of artificial intelligence to deliver customized strategic guidance on a continuous basis \- at a cost that SMBs can actually afford.

Corduroy starts with an in-depth kickoff session to establish a business’ goals, metrics, and trajectories. It then conducts an in-depth data audit of all the various sources available, from internal spreadsheets and document files to 3rd party accounting software, CRMs, and marketing systems. All that data is collected into Corduroy's secure data environment and is used by Corduroy’s expert strategic consultants to generate detailed insights, analysis, and 90-day strategic plan to make tangible impacts on business performance. 

The Corduroy product also prepares a detailed, structured, week-by-week plan tailored to the specific business. A real-time dashboard tracks progress against that plan, and a daily coaching feature keeps the team accountable and on track. Experienced human consultants remain in the loop to review progress, ensure plans stay relevant, and intervene when a situation calls for genuine human judgment.

Corduroy is not a one-time engagement or a static tool. It is a continuously evolving strategic relationship \- one that grows with the business, adapts as conditions change, and shows up every day rather than once a quarter.

# How It Works

Every Corduroy engagement begins with a dedicated real-time working session with the company’s senior consultants. In this kickoff meeting, they work directly with business leadership to surface and document the assumptions, goals, and constraints that will shape the entire engagement. Nothing is templated or assumed \- the work starts with the business as it actually is, not as a generic case study.

From there, Corduroy’s consultants extract and organize relevant data from the business’s existing documents and systems \- financials, pipeline data, operational records, and more \- into a secure, dedicated repository in the cloud. That data becomes the foundation for a detailed strategic analysis across key dimensions: financial health, operational efficiency, growth trajectory, and strategic positioning. The result is a comprehensive Growth and Data Audit delivered as a structured, actionable deliverable package \- not a slide deck, but a working document the business can actually use.

Based on the audit, Corduroy establishes 90-day goals and measurable KPIs tailored to the specific business. It then synthesizes the analysis into a clear, week-by-week roadmap that tells the team not just what to do, but in what order and why. Each milestone is broken down into actionable steps that don’t require an MBA to follow.

From that point forward, the Corduroy platform becomes part of the daily rhythm of the business: Leadership accesses a live dashboard that shows progress against the plan, allowing them to identify areas to focus on and drive their activities on a operational basis. Each day, the platform’s coaching layer sends reminders, tracks completions, flags warning areas, and surfaces emerging issues before they become problems, keeping the team focused on what matters most.

Behind the scenes, experienced human consultants monitor the progress against the recommendations. They review updates and the latest data from various sources for accuracy and relevance, update plans as the business evolves, and step in directly when a situation calls for nuanced human judgment: a major pivot, an unexpected market shift, a sensitive operational decision. The customer experiences this as a seamless advisor on their desktop: the intelligence of a savvy, human advisor, with ongoing, accessible, visualizations, advice, and input, available whenever they need it.
